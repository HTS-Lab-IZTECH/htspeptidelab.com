HTS Lab Receives TÜBİTAK Research Grant

We are proud to share that the HTS Lab has been awarded a prestigious TÜBİTAK research grant. This funding will significantly advance our capacity to conduct cutting-edge research in peptide and protein engineering.

The grant will support our investigative efforts into molecular interactions, enabling both computational simulations and experimental validation of designed therapeutic peptides. The project is expected to run over 36 months and will involve collaborations with national and international institutions.

This recognition underscores the scientific excellence and innovation at the core of our lab's mission. We are grateful to TÜBİTAK for supporting our work.
