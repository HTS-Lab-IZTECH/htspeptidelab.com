Lab Member Receives Best Poster Award at National Congress

It is with great pride that we congratulate one of our graduate students for being honored with the Best Poster Award at the National Bioengineering Congress. This achievement reflects the hard work, scientific rigor, and dedication that defines the spirit of our lab.

The awarded poster presented novel findings on the dynamics of protein folding under different physiological conditions, combining molecular dynamics simulations with experimental validation. The judges praised both the scientific depth and the clarity of presentation.

This recognition is a testament to the talent within the HTS Lab and motivates us all to continue pushing the boundaries of bioengineering research.
