We Are Recruiting! PhD and Master's Positions Available

The HTS Lab at IZTECH is currently looking to recruit highly motivated Master's and PhD students to join our growing research team. We are seeking candidates with strong backgrounds in bioengineering, biochemistry, molecular biology, or computational sciences.

Available positions include projects focused on peptide library design, protein interaction characterization, and molecular dynamics simulations. Successful candidates will receive mentorship, access to state-of-the-art facilities, and opportunities for international collaboration.

If you are excited about pioneering work at the intersection of computational and experimental biology, please visit our Positions page for more details and application instructions.
