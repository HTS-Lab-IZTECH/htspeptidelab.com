HTS Lab Presents at International Bioengineering Symposium

Members of the HTS Lab had the honor of presenting their research at the International Bioengineering Symposium. The oral presentation highlighted our recent progress in computational and experimental characterization of protein interactions.

The symposium brought together leading researchers from across the globe and provided a great opportunity for networking and scientific exchange. Our team received excellent feedback on our novel approach to peptide engineering for therapeutic applications.

We look forward to seeing the collaborations and ideas that emerge from this meeting.
