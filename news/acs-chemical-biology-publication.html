New Paper Published in ACS Chemical Biology

We are excited to share that our research group has published a new paper in ACS Chemical Biology. The paper, titled "Engineering Bioactive Peptides with Enhanced Target Selectivity via Computational Design," presents a new framework for peptide optimization.

Using a combination of in silico design strategies and biochemical assays, our team demonstrated a significant improvement in binding selectivity for therapeutic target proteins. This work is expected to have broad implications for the development of precision diagnostics and next-generation therapeutics.

The paper is now available online. We encourage interested researchers to visit our Publications page for the full citation and DOI link.
