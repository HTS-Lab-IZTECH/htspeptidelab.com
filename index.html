Our Mission at IZTECH

Located at the Izmir Institute of Technology's Bioengineering Department, we perform both computational and experimental characterization of protein interactions with themselves, other proteins, and small molecules. Optimizing these interactions ensures the efficient functioning of carefully designed molecular systems.
