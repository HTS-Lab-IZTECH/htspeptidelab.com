Dr. Hümeyra Taşkent Sezgin leads the Peptide & Protein Engineering Research Group at the Izmir Institute of Technology Bioengineering Department. Her extensive research bridges experimental and computational characterization of complex molecular interactions.

Her current work focuses on identifying key motifs in protein-protein interactions and utilizing advanced biochemical and biophysical techniques to understand these structures in depth.
